Gathering Materials

We first collected all the materials needed for the project. Wood, nails, screws, and bolts were picked up from Home Depot, while a used couch was found on the side of the street. Additional tools were borrowed from the garage, and the wheels for the cart were ordered online from Amazon.

Go-kart EV Couch

The frame of the cart was constructed by carefully cutting the wood to the right sizes. The pieces were nailed together to form a sturdy base, and the four non-motorized wheels were attached to the corners, allowing the frame to move freely in any direction.

Go-kart EV Couch

The wheels with built-in motors were wired by soldering connections to ensure proper power flow. Attention was paid to make sure the wiring was secure and well-insulated to avoid any short circuits during operation.

Go-kart EV Couch

Finally, all components were assembled together. The couch was mounted on the wooden frame, wheels and motor wiring were double-checked, and the cart was tested to confirm that everything functioned smoothly as a complete unit.
